Biography

Arshad Nadeem is a Pakistani javelin thrower known for his remarkable performances in international competitions. Born on January 2, 1997, in Mian Channu, Pakistan, he has represented his country in multiple events, including the Olympics, Commonwealth Games, and Asian Games. Arshad is celebrated for his dedication to the sport and his consistent achievements.

Achievements

  • Gold Medal - 2023 World Athletics Championships
  • Gold Medal - 2022 Commonwealth Games
  • Bronze Medal - 2018 Asian Games
  • First Pakistani to qualify for the Olympic finals in javelin throw
